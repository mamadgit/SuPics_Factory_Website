Cinema, throughout the history of art theory, has been known not only as the seventh art but also as the factory of images; a phrase that reveals its dual essence , an art born from imagination, and an industry born of order. SU was born from this very vision. In Persian, “SU” is a word rich with layers of meaning. it unites Light, as the power to reveal; Water, as the rhythm of life; and Direction, as the path through the unseen. This trinity shapes our philosophy: every image must bring light, carry life, and open a path for reflection. We believe that creativity without structure is wasted, and structure without creativity leads to repetition.

Factory of Images | SU

Is a pursuit to bring these two principles together: Creativity as the source of inspiration, and Order as the guarantee of fulfillment. From the first spark of an idea to the very last frame, each project evolves within a framework that is transparent, traceable, and faithful to both aesthetic values and quality standards. SU is a movement connecting art and industry,an endeavor to elevate image production from a mere commercial product to a cultural experience, one that is as effective as it is inspiring. This is the beginning of a journey, a motion rooted in contemporary culture, seeking to shape the future of the image not merely as a commodity,but as a living and artistic legacy.

About Us

SU is a creative and multidisciplinary studio specializing in filmmaking, advertising, and visual content production. With a young and skilled team, SU delivers a wide range of professional services, including:

  • Production of industrial and documentary films
  • Design and execution of advertising teasers and campaigns
  • Motion graphics and animation production
  • Visual coverage of events and projects

Our approach is founded on combining artistic vision with the commercial needs of each client, integrating creativity with innovative problem-solving to craft works that are lasting, influential, and aligned with international standards.

In addition, SU harnesses artificial intelligence and in-depth organizational analysis to offer intelligent solutions that optimize processes, enhance efficiency, and accelerate workflow.

This unique combination of art, technology, and organizational intelligence enables us to create projects that are not only visually compelling and impactful, but also capable of improving performance and productivity within our clients’ organizations.
